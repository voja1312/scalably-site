Our Specialized Services

At Tulsa Spine and Rehab, we provide comprehensive chiropractic care tailored to your unique needs. Our integrated approach focuses on treating the root cause for long-term wellness.

Spinal Adjustments

Precise manual adjustments to realign the spine, reduce pressure on nerves, and restore proper function to the nervous system.

Sports Rehabilitation

Specialized care for athletes and active individuals, including golf-related injuries with Titleist Performance Institute certification.

Integrated Wellness

Holistic approach combining chiropractic care with complementary therapies for complete musculoskeletal health.

Personalized Treatment Plans

Custom-designed therapeutic programs addressing your specific condition, lifestyle, and health goals.

Therapeutic Exercise

Targeted strength and flexibility exercises to support recovery, prevent reinjury, and enhance overall function.

Specialized Care

About Dr. Sean Riley

Dr. Sean Riley brings exceptional expertise to Tulsa Spine and Rehab with his comprehensive background in both Zoology and Chiropractic. His specialized knowledge in spinal and musculoskeletal care allows him to offer patients truly customized treatment plans.

Drawing on years of experience and advanced training, Dr. Riley focuses on integrated care that addresses not just symptoms but underlying causes. His approach combines precise manual adjustments with therapeutic exercises designed for long-term recovery and wellness.

As a certified specialist with the Titleist Performance Institute, Dr. Riley also offers specialized treatment for golf-related injuries. Additionally, he proudly serves patients at the Cancer Treatment Centers of America in Tulsa, providing supportive care during cancer treatment.
