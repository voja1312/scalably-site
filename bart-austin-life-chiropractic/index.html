Dr. Bart Patzer

3rd Generation Upper Cervical Specialist

Dr. Bart Patzer carries forward a proud family legacy in upper cervical chiropractic care, following in the footsteps of both his father and grandfather. With over 20 years of dedicated service to the Austin community, he brings unparalleled expertise and passion to his practice.

Specializing in the Orthospinology procedure, Dr. Patzer uses precise x-ray analysis to deliver gentle, specific adjustments that restore optimal nervous system function. His approach has helped patients of all ages—from newborns to seniors over 100—find relief from a wide range of conditions without drugs or invasive procedures.

Dr. Patzer believes that true healing comes from within when the body's natural communication systems function properly. His goal is to remove interference in the nervous system, allowing your body to heal itself as it was designed to do.
