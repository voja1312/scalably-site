About Dr. Paul Harris

Dr. Harris is the Owner and President of Harris Sports Chiropractic & Wellness Center, proudly serving Jenks, Oklahoma since 1986. With over 39 years of experience in the field, he has dedicated his career to helping patients achieve optimal health through natural chiropractic care.

Having treated thousands of patients throughout his career, Dr. Harris combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern approaches to provide comprehensive care tailored to each individual's needs.
