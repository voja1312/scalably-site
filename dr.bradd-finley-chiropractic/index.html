Dr. Brad Finley

Dr. Brad Finley is a dedicated chiropractor with over 15 years of experience serving the Tulsa community. His patient-centered approach focuses on creating personalized treatment plans that address the root cause of pain and discomfort, not just the symptoms.

After completing his doctorate at Parker University College of Chiropractic in Dallas, Dr. Finley returned to his hometown of Tulsa to provide compassionate, evidence-based care to his community. He regularly pursues continuing education to stay at the forefront of chiropractic advances.

Credentials & Specializations

  • Doctor of Chiropractic, Parker University
  • Certified in Active Release Techniques (ART)
  • Sports Chiropractic Certification
  • Member, American Chiropractic Association
  • Oklahoma Chiropractic Association Board Member
