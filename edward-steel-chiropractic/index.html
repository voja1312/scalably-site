Meet Dr. Edward Steel

Expert Chiropractor specializing in Sports Medicine

Dr. Edward Steel has been providing exceptional chiropractic care in Charlotte for over 40 years. After graduating from the prestigious Logan College of Chiropractic in 1982, he established Steel Chiropractic with a mission to help patients achieve optimal health through non-invasive, holistic approaches.

With specialized training in sports medicine, Dr. Steel has worked with athletes of all levels - from weekend warriors to professional competitors. His approach combines traditional chiropractic techniques with cutting-edge rehabilitation methods to deliver personalized care for each patient's unique needs.
