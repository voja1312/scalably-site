Meet Dr. Andy Harding

Born and raised in Kansas City, Kansas, Dr. Andy Harding has dedicated over 30 years to providing exceptional chiropractic care to the community. As the sixth of six children, he learned early the values of compassion, dedication, and service that now define his practice.

After attending St. Patrick's grade school and graduating from Bishop Ward High School in 1981, Dr. Harding earned a football scholarship to Kansas State University. There, he excelled both athletically as a 4-year letterman and academically, receiving Academic All-Big Eight awards in 1985. His educational journey culminated with a degree in Human Movement Studies from KSU and graduation from Cleveland Chiropractic College in 1989.

Since establishing his practice, Dr. Harding has been recognized as "Chiropractor of the Year" over 15 times by various publications, a testament to his commitment to patient care and professional excellence. His leadership in the field includes numerous positions with the International Chiropractors Association and being named a Distinguished Fellow of the ICA.

In 2011, Dr. Harding founded the Band of Brothers in KCK, mentoring Chiropractors and students worldwide. His passion for sharing chiropractic philosophy has led him to speak to audiences across 12 states and internationally through virtual presentations.
