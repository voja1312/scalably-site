Dr. Rick Bewley

With over 35 years of experience, Dr. Rick Bewley has established himself as a leading chiropractor in Tulsa, Oklahoma. His practice focuses on providing holistic, personalized care that addresses the root causes of pain and discomfort.

Dr. Bewley combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern approaches to deliver the most effective treatment for each patient's unique needs. His commitment to continuing education ensures that his patients receive the most up-to-date care available in the field.
