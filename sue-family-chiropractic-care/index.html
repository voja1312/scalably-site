About Dr. Sue Rance

Dr. Sue Rance is a dedicated chiropractor committed to helping patients restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve their overall quality of life through personalized chiropractic care.

With years of specialized training and experience, Dr. Rance works with patients of all ages - from infants to seniors - offering personalized care plans tailored to each individual's specific needs and health goals.

Her approach combines hands-on chiropractic adjustments with comprehensive lifestyle guidance to support long-term wellness and prevent future issues. Dr. Rance believes in treating the whole person, not just symptoms, and empowering patients to take an active role in their health journey.

Our Services

Spinal Adjustments

Gentle, precise adjustments to correct spinal misalignments, relieve pressure on nerves, and restore proper function to your body's systems.

Pediatric Chiropractic

Safe, gentle care for infants and children to support proper development, address common childhood conditions, and build a foundation for lifelong health.

Sports Injury Treatment

Specialized care for athletes of all levels to recover from injuries, improve performance, and prevent future injuries through proper biomechanics.

Prenatal Chiropractic

Safe, gentle adjustments to help manage pregnancy-related discomfort, maintain pelvic alignment, and prepare for a more comfortable delivery.

Headache Relief

Effective care for tension headaches and migraines by addressing the root causes, including neck tension, spinal misalignments, and lifestyle factors.

Nutritional Guidance

Personalized nutritional advice to complement your chiropractic care, reduce inflammation, support healing, and enhance your overall wellness.
