About Us

Dr. Brian Flournoy

Dr. Brian Flournoy is the founder and lead chiropractor at Performance Care Clinic in Tulsa, Oklahoma. With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Flournoy has dedicated his career to helping patients achieve optimal health through holistic, patient-focused approaches to healing.

At Performance Care Clinic, we believe in treating the body as a whole rather than just addressing isolated symptoms. Our mission is to facilitate recovery and restore movement and comfort quickly while providing personalized care for each individual's unique needs.

Doctor of Chiropractic, Parker University
Board Certified Chiropractor
Advanced Training in Sports Medicine
Member, American Chiropractic Association
