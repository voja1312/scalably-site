About Dr. John Emerzian

Dr. Emerzian is a bilingual chiropractor helping patients find long-term relief through personalized care, spinal alignment, and posture-focused treatment. With extensive training from Cleveland Chiropractic College, he combines clinical expertise with a compassionate approach to healing.

His comprehensive treatment methods address the root causes of pain rather than just managing symptoms, enabling patients to return to their active lifestyles with confidence.
