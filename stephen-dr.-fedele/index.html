Meet Dr. Fedele

Dr. Fedele is a seasoned chiropractor providing expert spinal care for generations of Fresno residents. With a career spanning over five decades, he has built a reputation for delivering effective, compassionate care that addresses the root cause of pain and discomfort.

Known for his gentle approach and deep expertise in spinal health, Dr. Fedele is dedicated to helping patients achieve long-term relief through personalized chiropractic treatment plans. His philosophy centers on treating the whole person, not just symptoms, empowering patients to take control of their health and wellness.
