Meet Dr. Janayy Scott

Dedicated to helping you achieve optimal health and wellness

Dr. Janayy Scott founded Essential Life Chiropractic & Wellness with a mission to help families throughout San Antonio experience the transformative benefits of principled chiropractic care.

With extensive training and a passion for family wellness, Dr. Scott provides specific chiropractic adjustments and personalized care tailored to each patient's unique goals - whether it's pain relief, improved function, or long-term wellbeing.

  • Family-focused chiropractic care for all ages
  • Personalized treatment plans for optimal results
  • Holistic approach addressing the root cause, not just symptoms
  • Commitment to ongoing education and advanced techniques
