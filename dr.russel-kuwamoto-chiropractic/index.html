Our Specialized Services

At Kuwamoto Chiropractic, we offer comprehensive care designed to address pain, improve function, and enhance your overall quality of life through natural, non-invasive techniques.

Spinal Adjustments

Precise, gentle adjustments to correct misalignments, reduce pain, and restore proper nervous system function.

Auto Accident Care

Specialized treatment for whiplash and other injuries resulting from automobile accidents, with complete documentation for insurance claims.

Pain Management

Comprehensive approach to managing chronic and acute pain conditions without relying on medication or invasive procedures.

Spinal Decompression

Non-surgical spinal decompression therapy to relieve pressure on compressed discs and nerves, ideal for herniated discs and sciatica.

Rehabilitative Exercises

Customized exercise programs to strengthen supporting muscles, improve flexibility, and prevent future injuries.

Wellness Education

Personalized guidance on ergonomics, nutrition, and lifestyle modifications to support long-term health and prevent recurring issues.

Dr. Russell Kuwamoto

With over four decades of dedicated practice, Dr. Russell Kuwamoto has established himself as one of Fresno's most trusted chiropractic specialists. As founder of Kuwamoto Chiropractic Clinic, he has helped thousands of patients recover from injuries, manage chronic conditions, and improve their overall quality of life.

Dr. Kuwamoto's approach combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern methodologies, always focusing on personalized care that addresses each patient's unique needs. His extensive training and commitment to continuing education ensure that his patients receive the most effective, evidence-based treatments available.

Education & Credentials

  • Training from International Sportsmedicine Institute in Los Angeles

  • Certified in Whiplash & Brain Traumatology
