Our Holistic Services

Our comprehensive approach to wellness includes a variety of treatments designed to help you live pain-free and achieve optimal health.

Chiropractic Adjustment

Expert spinal adjustments to restore proper alignment, improve function, and relieve pain through gentle, precise techniques.

Physical Therapy

Customized therapy programs to strengthen muscles, improve mobility, and accelerate recovery from injuries or chronic conditions.

Family Medicine

Comprehensive primary care services from our Family Nurse Practitioner expertise, focusing on prevention and whole-person wellness.

Health Assessment

Thorough evaluation of your condition using advanced diagnostic techniques to develop targeted treatment strategies.

Natural Healing

Evidence-based natural treatments and supplements to support your body's innate healing abilities and overall wellness.

Wellness Consultation

Meet Dr. Russell Gilstrap

Since 1987, Dr. Gilstrap has been a trusted healthcare provider in the Tulsa metropolitan area, combining his expertise as both a Chiropractic Physician and Family Nurse Practitioner to deliver exceptional care.

With over three decades of experience, Dr. Gilstrap provides a unique integrated approach to healthcare, addressing not just symptoms but root causes of health issues. His dual credentials allow him to offer comprehensive treatment options that bridge traditional medicine with natural healing methods.
