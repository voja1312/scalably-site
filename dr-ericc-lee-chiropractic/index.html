About Dr. Eric Allen Lee

Dedicated Chiropractor with a Focus on Patient-Centered Care

Dr. Eric Allen Lee DC is a skilled chiropractor serving the Fresno community with personalized care and evidence-based treatment approaches. With years of experience in chiropractic medicine, Dr. Lee is committed to helping patients achieve optimal health and wellness through non-invasive, holistic treatment methods.

His approach focuses on identifying the root cause of pain and discomfort, creating customized treatment plans that address your unique needs and health goals.

  • Personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific condition
  • Gentle, effective adjustment techniques for all ages
  • Focus on long-term solutions rather than temporary relief
  • Comprehensive approach addressing lifestyle and wellness factors

Professional Credentials

  • Doctor of Chiropractic
  • Licensed by the California Board of Chiropractic Examiners
  • Advanced Training in Spinal Correction
  • Certified in Sports Injury Management
  • Member of the California Chiropractic Association
  • Continuing Education in Latest Treatment Techniques
