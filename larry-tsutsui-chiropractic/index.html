Dr. Larry Masuo Tsutsui DC

Dr. Tsutsui is a highly skilled chiropractor dedicated to helping patients achieve optimal wellness through natural, non-invasive treatments. Since graduating from Palmer College of Chiropractic in 1984, he has been serving the Fresno community with personalized, evidence-based care.

His approach focuses on restoring proper alignment to the spine, relieving pain, and improving overall function without medications or surgery. Dr. Tsutsui believes in treating the whole person, not just symptoms, and works with each patient to develop a customized treatment plan.

Our Specialized Services

Dr. Tsutsui offers a comprehensive range of chiropractic treatments tailored to your specific needs and concerns.

Spinal Adjustments

Gentle, precise spinal manipulations to restore proper alignment, improve mobility, and alleviate pain. Dr. Tsutsui uses various techniques tailored to your comfort level and specific condition.

Learn More

Lower Back Pain

Effective treatment for acute and chronic lower back pain, including sciatica, herniated discs, and muscular strain. Our approach addresses both symptoms and underlying causes.

Learn More

Neck Pain Relief

Specialized care for neck pain resulting from poor posture, whiplash, tech neck, and degenerative conditions. Treatment focuses on pain relief and long-term prevention.

Learn More

Hip Pain Treatment

Comprehensive approach to hip pain, addressing joint dysfunction, muscle imbalances, and related issues. Treatment improves mobility and reduces discomfort in daily activities.

Learn More

Scoliosis Management

Specialized care for scoliosis patients of all ages. Our treatment plans help manage curvature progression, reduce pain, and improve function through targeted adjustments and exercises.

Wellness Care
