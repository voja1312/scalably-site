About Dr. Wong

Experienced Chiropractic Care You Can Trust

Dr. Steven Wong is a licensed chiropractor with over 15 years of experience in providing holistic chiropractic care to patients in Fresno, California. After graduating with honors from Palmer College of Chiropractic, Dr. Wong has dedicated his career to helping patients achieve optimal health through natural, non-invasive treatments.

His approach combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern treatment modalities, customized to each patient's unique needs and health goals.
