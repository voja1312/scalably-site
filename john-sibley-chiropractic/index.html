About Dr. John Sibley

Dr. John Sibley, D.C.

Advanced Chiropractic & Acupuncture Expert

Dr. Sibley is a distinguished chiropractor with over two decades of dedicated practice in holistic healing. His journey to chiropractic excellence began with his own experience finding relief from severe headaches through chiropractic care, inspiring him to leave a career in corporate petroleum research to pursue his passion for natural healing.

Today, Dr. Sibley's clinic in Tulsa stands as a benchmark of excellence in chiropractic and acupuncture care, known for its personalized treatment approaches and exceptional patient outcomes.
