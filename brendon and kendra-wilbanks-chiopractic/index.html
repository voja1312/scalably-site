Meet Your Doctors

Dr. Brandon and Dr. Kendra Wilbanks are a husband-and-wife team and co-owners of Wilbanks Chiropractic in Tulsa, Oklahoma. They run the clinic together and bring a complementary blend of expertise to serve patients of all ages.

Dr. Brandon Wilbanks

Doctor of Chiropractic, ART Certified

A Tulsa native and Union High School graduate, Dr. Brandon earned a degree in Nutritional Sciences from Oklahoma State University before completing his Doctor of Chiropractic degree at Parker University in 2011. He is certified in ART - Active Release Techniques - the gold standard for treating soft tissue, nerve, and muscle conditions. His approach focuses on corrective spinal adjustments and targeted soft tissue therapy.

Doctor of Chiropractic ART Certified Nutritional Sciences Parker University '11

Dr. Kendra Wilbanks

Doctor of Chiropractic, Prenatal & Pediatric Specialist

Originally from Searcy, Arkansas, Dr. Kendra studied at Arkansas State University and also graduated from Parker University in 2011. She specializes in prenatal and pediatric chiropractic care and is passionate about helping families live healthier lives. Her gentle approach makes her a favorite among expecting mothers and children.

Doctor of Chiropractic Prenatal Specialist Pediatric Specialist Parker University '11
