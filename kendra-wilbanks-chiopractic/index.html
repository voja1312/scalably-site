Meet Your Chiropractors

Dr. Brandon & Dr. Kendra Wilbanks are a husband-and-wife team and co-owners of Wilbanks Chiropractic in Tulsa, Oklahoma. They run the clinic together and bring a complementary blend of expertise to serve patients of all ages.

Dr. Brandon Wilbanks, a Tulsa native and Union High School graduate, earned a degree in Nutritional Sciences from Oklahoma State University before completing his Doctor of Chiropractic degree at Parker University in 2011. He is certified in ART (Active Release Techniques), the gold standard for treating soft tissue, nerve, and muscle conditions. His approach focuses on corrective spinal adjustments and targeted soft tissue therapy.

Dr. Kendra Wilbanks, originally from Searcy, Arkansas, studied at Arkansas State University and also graduated from Parker University in 2011. She specializes in prenatal and pediatric chiropractic care and is passionate about helping families live healthier lives.
