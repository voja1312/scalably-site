Dr. Joe Martin D.C.

Sports Chiropractor / Human Performance Specialist

30+ Years Experience Military Medicine Sports Rehabilitation Performance Optimization

With over three decades of experience, Dr. Joe Martin has dedicated his career to elevating human performance through personalized chiropractic care and functional rehabilitation. As the founder of Martin Functional Rehabilitation and Head of Military and Sports Medicine Education at TRX/Fitness Anywhere Inc., Dr. Martin brings unparalleled expertise to every patient.

Dr. Martin earned his Doctor of Chiropractic with a specialization in Sport Rehabilitation from Life Chiropractic College West in 1990. His unique approach combines advanced chiropractic techniques with functional movement principles to not just treat injuries but to optimize overall human performance.

Our Specialized Services

Sports Chiropractic

Specialized care for athletes focused on optimizing performance, preventing injuries, and facilitating faster recovery times through targeted adjustments and rehabilitation protocols.

Functional Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehabilitation programs that restore proper movement patterns and rebuild strength after injury, focusing on whole-body functionality rather than isolated symptoms.

Injury Recovery

Accelerate healing and return to activity with personalized recovery plans combining manual therapy, corrective exercises, and progressive rehabilitation strategies.

Performance Optimization

Take your physical capabilities to the next level with customized performance enhancement protocols designed to identify and eliminate movement restrictions and imbalances.

Military & Sports Medicine

Specialized care for military personnel and athletes drawing on Dr. Martin's extensive experience developing programs for elite performers in demanding physical environments.

Mobility & Strength Plans

Customized training programs that balance mobility and strength development, addressing your specific limitations and goals to create sustainable physical resilience.
