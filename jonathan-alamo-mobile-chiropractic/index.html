About Dr. Jonathan Todd

Licensed Chiropractor Bringing Care to Your Door

Dr. Jonathan Todd is a licensed mobile chiropractor bringing the old-fashioned house call back to San Antonio and surrounding communities. With a passion for making quality chiropractic care accessible to everyone, Dr. Todd delivers expert treatment directly to your home, office, or athletic facility.

Unlike traditional chiropractic offices, Alamo Mobile Chiropractic eliminates the need for travel, waiting rooms, and scheduling hassles. Dr. Todd offers personalized, one-on-one care in the environment where you're most comfortable.

With experience treating patients of all ages and conditions, Dr. Todd specializes in creating individualized treatment plans that address your specific needs and lifestyle.
