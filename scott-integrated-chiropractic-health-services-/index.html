About Your Doctor

Dr. Scott Smith, DC

Dr. Scott Smith is the founder and lead chiropractor at Integrated Chiropractic Health Services and Modern Massage in Scottsdale, Arizona. With a passion for holistic health and wellness, Dr. Smith has dedicated his career to helping patients achieve optimal health through natural, non-invasive treatments.

His approach focuses on treating the whole person—not just symptoms—by identifying and addressing the root causes of pain and discomfort that are often linked to spinal imbalances. Dr. Smith combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern therapies to create personalized treatment plans that promote long-term wellness.
