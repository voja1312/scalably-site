Dr. Chad Owen

Your Path to Wellness Starts Here

As a second-generation chiropractor, Dr. Chad Owen has been serving the San Antonio community since 2000. Following in the footsteps of his father and uncle, Dr. Owen knew from a young age that chiropractic care was his calling.

After graduating from Parker College of Chiropractic, he returned to work alongside his father and has since built a thriving family-centered practice focused on holistic health and wellness.

Professional Certifications

  • Applied Kinesiology - Advanced muscle testing techniques
  • Active Release Techniques - Soft tissue treatment specialist
  • Brimhall Technique - Comprehensive wellness approach
  • Studied under leading experts including Dr. George Goodheart and Dr. Jeff Spencer (Lance Armstrong's sports chiropractor)
