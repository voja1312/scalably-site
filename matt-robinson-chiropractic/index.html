Our Specialized Services

Dr. Robinson provides a comprehensive range of chiropractic techniques and physical therapies tailored to your specific needs. As an experienced "old skool" chiropractor, he focuses on effective care without unnecessary long treatment plans.

Chiropractic Adjustments

Expert spinal adjustments to restore proper alignment, relieve pain, and improve your body's natural ability to heal itself.

Auto Accident Care

Specialized treatment for injuries resulting from auto accidents with flexible payment options. Dr. Robinson will wait for payment in car accident cases.

Personalized Treatment

Physical Therapy

Complementary physical therapy techniques to enhance your recovery, strengthen muscles, and prevent future injuries.

Insurance Assistance

Wellness Care

Preventative care and maintenance to keep your spine healthy and your body functioning optimally for years to come.
