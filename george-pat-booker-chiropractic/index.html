15+ Years Experience

Dr. George Rodriguez, DC

Dr. Rodriguez is a committed chiropractor who has dedicated his career to helping patients find relief from pain and improve their quality of life. With over 15 years of experience, he combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern approaches to provide personalized care for each patient.

His holistic approach focuses on treating the root cause of pain rather than just the symptoms, enabling patients to achieve lasting relief and improved function. Dr. Rodriguez believes in empowering his patients through education about their conditions and involving them in their healing journey.

Doctor of Chiropractic, Parker University
Board Certified by the Texas Board of Chiropractic Examiners
Advanced Training in Spinal Rehabilitation
