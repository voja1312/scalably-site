Meet Your Chiropractor

Dr. Shawnie Lamborn

With a passion for helping patients live powerful, whole, and healthy lives, Dr. Shawnie founded Chiro-Fitness Inc. based on the philosophy that chiropractic care plays a key role in sports, fitness, and overall health.
