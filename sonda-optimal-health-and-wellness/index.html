Our Story

Since 2004, Drs. Riley and Sonda Powell have been dedicated to helping patients of all ages achieve optimal health through chiropractic care. Originally operating as Powell Chiropractic, they rebranded to Optimal Health and Wellness in 2018 to better reflect their expanded focus on holistic health solutions.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to remove interference so the body can heal itself. We believe in addressing the root cause of health issues rather than just treating symptoms, empowering our patients to live their healthiest lives possible.
