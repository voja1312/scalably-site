About Dr. Bennett

Dedicated to Your Health & Wellness

Dr. Jared Bennett has over 16 years of experience providing personalized chiropractic treatment for spine alignment, posture correction, and pain relief in Tulsa, Oklahoma. His patient-centered approach focuses on addressing the root causes of pain and discomfort, not just treating symptoms.

With extensive training in advanced chiropractic techniques, Dr. Bennett is committed to helping patients achieve optimal health through natural, non-invasive care that promotes the body's innate healing abilities.
