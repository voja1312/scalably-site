Our Specialized Services

We take a comprehensive approach to chiropractic care, addressing the root causes of your discomfort rather than just treating symptoms.

Spinal Adjustment

Precise manual adjustments to realign your spine, relieve pressure on nerves, and restore proper function to your entire nervous system.

Personalized Treatment Plans

Custom wellness plans tailored to your specific needs, addressing the unique challenges of your body and lifestyle.

Headache & Migraine Relief

Specialized techniques to reduce tension and frequency of headaches, addressing the underlying spinal and nervous system causes.

Posture Correction

Assessment and correction of postural issues that may be contributing to pain, discomfort, and reduced mobility.

Sports Injury Treatment

Specialized care for athletes of all levels, focusing on injury recovery, performance enhancement, and prevention.

Therapeutic Massage

Complementary massage therapy to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, and enhance the effectiveness of adjustments.

Meet Your Chiropractor

Dr. Jason Shenloogian

Dr. Shenloogian is dedicated to delivering exceptional chiropractic care to the Tulsa community. His approach focuses on identifying and treating the root causes of pain and discomfort, rather than simply addressing symptoms.

With years of specialized training and experience, Dr. Shenloogian has developed a holistic treatment philosophy that considers all aspects of a patient's health and lifestyle. He believes that proper spinal alignment is fundamental to overall wellness, affecting everything from pain levels to immune function.
