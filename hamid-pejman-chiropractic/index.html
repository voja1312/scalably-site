About Dr. Hamid Pejman DC

Dr. Hamid Pejman is a highly skilled chiropractor with extensive experience in treating a wide range of conditions related to joints, nerves, and musculoskeletal health. His approach combines evidence-based techniques with a patient-focused philosophy to promote faster recovery and lasting relief.

After completing his Doctor of Chiropractic degree, Dr. Pejman has dedicated his career to helping patients overcome pain and improve their quality of life through non-invasive, personalized care plans tailored to each individual's unique needs.

His compassionate approach and commitment to continued education in the latest chiropractic techniques have helped countless patients in the San Antonio area achieve optimal health and wellness.
