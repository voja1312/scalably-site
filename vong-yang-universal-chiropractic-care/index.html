Meet Dr. Vong Yang

Dr. Vong Yang is a highly skilled chiropractor and massage therapist with extensive experience in relieving chronic pain, tension, and stress through personalized techniques and holistic healing approaches.

Based in Clovis, California, Dr. Yang has dedicated his career to helping patients achieve optimal wellness through non-invasive, natural treatments that address the root causes of discomfort and dysfunction.

With a compassionate approach to patient care, Dr. Yang creates a calming, welcoming environment where healing begins the moment you walk through the door. His treatments are tailored to each individual's unique needs, ensuring the most effective path to recovery and wellness.
