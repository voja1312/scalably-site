Our Specialized Services

Dr. Gomez combines advanced chiropractic techniques with sports medicine expertise to provide comprehensive care for both athletes and everyday patients.

Low Back Pain Relief

Targeted manipulation and muscle work to address the root causes of low back pain, from disc issues to muscle imbalances.

Migraine Treatment

Specialized techniques to reduce the frequency and intensity of migraines by addressing cervical spine alignment and tension.

Plantar Fasciitis Care

Comprehensive treatment for heel pain through soft tissue work, adjustments, and customized stretching protocols.

Tennis Elbow Treatment

Evidence-based approaches to reduce inflammation and promote healing in the lateral epicondyle for lasting relief.

Golfer's Elbow Relief

Specialized care for medial epicondylitis, combining adjustments with targeted rehabilitation exercises.

Sports Performance

Enhance athletic performance through proper biomechanics, joint mobility, and specialized Titleist Performance Institute techniques.

About Dr. Paul Gomez

Dr. Paul Gomez, DC

Logan College of Chiropractic Applied Kinesiology Rock Tape Motion Palpation Activator Titleist Performance

As a second-generation chiropractor, Dr. Paul Gomez brings both family legacy and modern expertise to his practice. After graduating from Berryhill High in 2002, he pursued his passion for healing at Logan College of Chiropractic, earning his Doctor of Chiropractic degree in 2015.

Treatment Philosophy

"I believe that true healing requires addressing both the joint dysfunction and the surrounding muscle tension. A joint cannot fully recover without treating the tight muscles restricting its movement, which is why my approach integrates multiple techniques for comprehensive care."

Dr. Gomez has been married to Anna for over 10 years and is the proud father of 19-year-old Andrew. His personal experience with sports-related injuries as a former athlete gives him unique insight into the recovery process and fuels his commitment to providing exceptional care.
