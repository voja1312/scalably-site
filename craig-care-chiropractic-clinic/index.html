About Dr. Willemstein

Dr. Willemstein Craig is a skilled chiropractor offering holistic and targeted treatment for sports injuries, chronic pain, and spinal misalignment. He integrates alternative medicine principles into every patient plan, ensuring a comprehensive approach to your health and wellness.

With years of experience in chiropractic care and a deep understanding of the body's natural healing processes, Dr. Craig is committed to helping his patients achieve optimal health and mobility without reliance on medications or invasive procedures.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of chiropractic treatments tailored to your specific needs

Spinal Adjustments

Precise manual adjustments to correct spinal misalignments, restore proper function, and alleviate nerve pressure for improved well-being.

Back Pain Relief

Targeted treatments to address acute and chronic back pain, helping you return to daily activities with reduced discomfort.

Neck Pain Therapy

Specialized techniques to alleviate neck tension, improve range of motion, and reduce pain from poor posture or injuries.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Customized rehabilitation programs to help athletes and active individuals recover from injuries and return to peak performance.

Postural Correction

Assessment and treatment to improve alignment, enhance body mechanics, and prevent future pain from poor posture.

Holistic Care

Integrative approach combining chiropractic adjustments with nutritional guidance and lifestyle recommendations for whole-body wellness.
