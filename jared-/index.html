Meet Dr. Jared Bennett, DC

For over 16 years, Dr. Bennett has been dedicated to providing exceptional chiropractic care to patients throughout Tulsa. His patient-centered approach combines evidence-based techniques with compassionate care to help patients achieve optimal health and wellness.

Dr. Bennett specializes in treating a wide range of conditions, from chronic back pain and sports injuries to headaches and postural problems. His commitment to continuing education ensures that his patients receive the most current and effective treatment options available.

Credentials & Education

  • Licensed Chiropractor, State of Oklahoma
  • 16 Years of Clinical Experience
  • Advanced Training in Sport Injury Rehabilitation
