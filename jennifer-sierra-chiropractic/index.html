Meet Our Doctors

Expert Chiropractic Care by Husband & Wife Team

At Sierra Chiropractic, we're dedicated to providing exceptional care that helps you achieve optimal health and wellness naturally. Our husband-and-wife team brings together decades of experience, advanced training, and a genuine passion for helping patients live pain-free lives.

What sets us apart is our personalized approach—we take the time to understand your unique health concerns and develop customized treatment plans that address the root cause of your problems, not just the symptoms.

Dr. Steven R. Mathews, D.C., CMT
Life Chiropractic College West Graduate

Dr. Steve has practiced chiropractic since 2001 and has been a certified massage therapist since 1995. His unique background includes eight years as a Fresno County paramedic before pursuing his chiropractic education, giving him exceptional insight into emergency care and patient needs.

Dr. Jennifer M. Mathews, D.C.
Fresno State & Life Chiropractic College West Graduate

Dr. Jen earned her biology-physiology degree from Fresno State in 1999 followed by her Doctor of Chiropractic from Life Chiropractic College West in 2002. She brings specialized expertise in family wellness care and women's health issues to our practice.
