Our Treatment Process

Understanding what to expect helps patients feel comfortable and confident in their care journey. Here's how we work with you to achieve optimal health outcomes.

1

Comprehensive Assessment

Your first visit includes a thorough health history review, physical examination, and discussion of your symptoms and goals. This gives us a complete picture of your condition and needs.

2

Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on your assessment, Dr. Martinez develops a customized treatment approach targeting your specific issues. We discuss all options and ensure you're comfortable with the recommended plan.

3

Active Treatment Phase

Through a series of targeted adjustments and therapies, we address the root causes of your pain or mobility issues. Each session builds on previous progress toward your health goals.

4

Progress Monitoring

We regularly evaluate your response to treatment and make adjustments as needed. This ensures optimal results and helps us address any new concerns promptly.

5

Wellness Maintenance

Once your primary concerns are resolved, we offer ongoing wellness care to maintain your improvements and prevent future issues through periodic checks and adjustments.
