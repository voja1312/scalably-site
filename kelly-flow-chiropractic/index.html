Meet Dr. Kelly Cogan

With over 25 years of experience and extensive training in multiple healing modalities, Dr. Cogan brings a wealth of knowledge to her practice.

Dr. Kelly Cogan, D.C.

Doctor of Chiropractic, Certified in Multiple Healing Modalities

Since graduating in 1997, Dr. Cogan has dedicated her career to expanding her knowledge of the body's natural healing capabilities. Her journey began at age 22 when she entered chiropractic school, and has since taken her through studies in diverse healing arts and around the world.

Dr. Cogan's international practice experience in Ireland, England, and Italy has shaped her unique approach, allowing her to connect with diverse cultures through the universal language of healing. After settling in Albuquerque, NM, where she built a thriving practice while studying Ayurvedic Medicine with renowned teacher Vasant Lad, she fulfilled her dream of moving to Portland in 2018.

Today, she welcomes patients to her peaceful home office overlooking a garden and the city, creating a serene environment for healing. Her gentle approach combines manual care with personalized home exercises to empower patients in their wellness journey.
