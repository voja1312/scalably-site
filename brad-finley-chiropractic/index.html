Our Specialized Services

At Finley Chiropractic, we offer a comprehensive range of treatments designed to address your specific needs and promote long-term wellness.

Spinal Adjustment

Our core chiropractic service corrects misalignments in your spine to relieve pain, improve mobility, and restore proper nerve function.

Sports Injury Therapy

Specialized care for athletes and active individuals to recover from injuries, enhance performance, and prevent future problems.

Percussion Therapy

Advanced deep tissue massage using percussive technology to reduce muscle tension, improve blood flow, and accelerate recovery.

Diagnostic Assessment

Comprehensive examination including posture analysis, range of motion testing, and when necessary, digital imaging to identify the root cause of your issues.

Nutritional Counseling

Personalized guidance on supplements and diet plans that support your body's natural healing process and overall wellness.

Posture Correction

Targeted exercises and adjustments to improve your posture, relieving strain on your spine and preventing chronic issues.

Meet Dr. Brad Finley

With over 15 years of experience in chiropractic care, Dr. Finley has dedicated his career to helping patients achieve optimal health through natural, non-invasive treatments. As a Tulsa native, he is passionate about serving his community and providing personalized care to each patient.

Dr. Finley combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern methodologies to deliver comprehensive care that addresses not just symptoms but the root causes of pain and discomfort.
